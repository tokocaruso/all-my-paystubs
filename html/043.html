All My Paystubs

August 12, 2015

remember that one day you forgot your PIN?
remember when you forgot your pen?
when your cousin got in a car crash but came out with a scratch?
when you ex made ‘headlines’?
when you read every book in the public library?
when you feel rude passing people on the street?
when you have an inner fight with yourself about whether to tell a stranger that there fly is down or there is something in their teeth? when you get arrested for making eye contact?
